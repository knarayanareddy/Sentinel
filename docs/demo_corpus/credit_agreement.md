## ARTICLE I - DEFINITIONS

**"Consolidated Total Debt"** means, as of any date of determination, the sum of (a) all Indebtedness for borrowed money of the Borrower and its Restricted Subsidiaries, and (b) all guaranteed Indebtedness for borrowed money of any Person by the Borrower or any Restricted Subsidiary, in each case as reflected on the consolidated balance sheet of the Borrower prepared in accordance with GAAP.

**"Consolidated EBITDA"** means, for any period, the sum of (a) Consolidated Net Income for such period plus (b) without duplication, the amount of deductions (but not additions) attributable to any of the following items (to the extent such items reduced Consolidated Net Income for such period): (i) interest expense, (ii) income tax expense, (iii) depreciation and amortization expense, and (iv) non-cash charges.

## ARTICLE IV - FINANCIAL COVENANTS

### §4.1 Quarterly Financial Certificates

The Borrower shall deliver to the Administrative Agent, within forty-five (45) days after the end of each fiscal quarter, a certificate of a Financial Officer certifying (a) that such Financial Officer has reviewed the financial statements delivered pursuant to Section 2.1(a)(ii) and (b) that, to such Financial Officer's knowledge, the Borrower is in compliance with the covenants of Article IV, or, if the Borrower is not in compliance with any such covenant, specifying the nature and extent of such non-compliance and the action taken, proposed to be taken or that is required to be taken to remedy such non-compliance.

### §4.2 Debt/EBITDA Covenant

The Borrower shall not permit the ratio of Consolidated Total Debt to Consolidated EBITDA for the most recently ended Test Period to exceed **4.50 to 1.00** (the "**Maximum Leverage Ratio**").

For purposes of this Section 4.2, the "**Test Period**" means the most recently completed four consecutive fiscal quarters of the Borrower for which financial statements are available.

**Covenant Compliance Certificate.** The Borrower shall deliver to the Administrative Agent, within five (5) Business Days after the end of each fiscal quarter, a certificate setting forth (a) a calculation of the ratio of Consolidated Total Debt to Consolidated EBITDA as of the last day of such fiscal quarter and for the Test Period then most recently ended, and (b) a certification by a Responsible Officer of the Borrower that such ratio does not exceed the Maximum Leverage Ratio.

**Event of Default.** Any breach of this Section 4.2 shall constitute an Event of Default under Section 7.1(a) hereof, and upon the occurrence and during the continuance of such Event of Default, the Administrative Agent shall, at the direction of the Required Lenders, declare all obligations of the Borrower hereunder immediately due and payable.

**Cure Period.** Notwithstanding the foregoing, if the Borrower fails to comply with the Maximum Leverage Ratio for any Test Period, the Borrower shall have thirty (30) days from the date of delivery of the Covenant Compliance Certificate for such Test Period to cure such non-compliance through (i) voluntary prepayment of Indebtedness, (ii) equity contributions by the Borrower's sponsors, or (iii) any combination thereof. If the Borrower fails to cure such non-compliance within such thirty (30) day period, an Event of Default shall be deemed to have occurred as of the last day of the Test Period for which such non-compliance was reported.

### §4.3 Interest Coverage Ratio

The Borrower shall not permit the ratio of Consolidated EBITDA to Consolidated Interest Expense for the most recently ended Test Period to be less than 2.00 to 1.00 (the "Minimum Interest Coverage Ratio").

### §4.4 Capital Expenditures

The Borrower shall not permit Consolidated Capital Expenditures for any fiscal year to exceed $75,000,000.

## ARTICLE VII - EVENTS OF DEFAULT

### §7.1 Events of Default

Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default":

**(a)** The Borrower fails to pay any principal of any Loan when due, whether at the stated maturity, by acceleration or otherwise, or fails to pay any interest on any Loan within three (3) Business Days after the due thereof;

**(b)** The Borrower fails to comply with any covenant set forth in Article IV (Financial Covenants) and such failure continues for more than thirty (30) days after written notice thereof from the Administrative Agent to the Borrower;

**(c)** The Borrower fails to comply with any other covenant or agreement contained in this Agreement or any Loan Document, and such failure continues for more than thirty (30) days after written notice thereof from the Administrative Agent to the Borrower;

**(d)** Any representation or warranty made by the Borrower in this Agreement or any Loan Document proves to have been false in any material respect when made;

**(e)** The Borrower or any Significant Subsidiary (i) files a voluntary petition in bankruptcy or is organized under the laws of a jurisdiction that permits or requires dissolution, (ii) is generally not paying its debts as they become due, or (iii) takes any action to approve a composition or arrangement with creditors.
